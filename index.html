how to post meme

  1. Enter image url, or click button.
  2. Enter upper text, or leave empty.
  3. Enter lower text, or leave empty.
  4. When ready, click button.

how to edit meme

  1. Hover mouse over meme, or tap on it.
  2. Click button that appears below it.
  3. Make changes, and preview image and text.
  4. When ready, click .

how to delete meme

  1. Hover over or tap on meme to edit.
  2. Click button that appears below it.
  3. In popup window, select OK to delete, or Cancel.

how to switch themes

  1. Click the black/white bar at top of screen.
  2. Click again to switch back to previous theme.
